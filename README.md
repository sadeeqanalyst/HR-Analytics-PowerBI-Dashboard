# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI using the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset.

The dashboard is designed to help HR teams monitor workforce trends, analyze employee attrition, evaluate performance, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

---

## Business Problem

Employee attrition has a direct impact on organizational performance and hiring costs. HR teams need a centralized dashboard to understand why employees leave, identify high-risk areas, and monitor workforce performance.

This dashboard provides meaningful insights into employee demographics, attrition patterns, salary distribution, and performance metrics to support better HR decisions.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across departments and job roles
- Monitor workforce demographics
- Evaluate employee performance and satisfaction
- Identify trends related to overtime and business travel
- Build an interactive dashboard for HR decision-making

---

## Key Insights

- Research & Development has the highest number of employee exits.
- Employees aged 26–35 contribute the largest share of attrition.
- Employees working overtime show a higher attrition rate.
- Sales Executive and Laboratory Technician roles experience higher turnover.
- Employee satisfaction and work-life balance are closely related to retention.
